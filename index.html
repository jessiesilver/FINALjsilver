INCARCERATION

Summary








At a glance

10,478,385 people are currently incarcerated, detained in a jail or prison against their will.

2,728,671 are waiting a trial and not yet convicted of a crime. The remaining 7,749,714 are serving a sentence -
for enaging in events that range from minor infringements of the law, to what are deemed serious moral transgressions.

3,117 are awaiting execution.


This Project

This project seeks to compare 10 settings in terms of incarceral systems and practices.
It uncovers current newsworthy events, and weighs statistics against national data.

Are there correlations between the wealth, wealth distribution, happiness and incarceration?
