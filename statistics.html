Statistics



How do these 10 countries compare in terms of statistics around incarceration?
Are there similarities, grave differences, correlations, trends?
Below, the countries are compared over 4 sets of statistics:

The rate of Incarcertion what percentage of the poputaion is incarcerated

The GDP per Capita the Gross Domestic Product of the country divided by the population

The Gini Index how evenly the total wealth of the country is spread among the population

Happiness per the World Happiness Report - the average happiness of the population

To compare the 10 countries, this data was processed such that all countries in the world were compared and awarded a percentile score based on their comparrison.
For example, because the US incarcerates the more of its popuation than any other nation in the world, it is in the 100th percentile for incarceration rate.
